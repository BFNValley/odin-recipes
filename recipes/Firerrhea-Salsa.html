Firerrhea Salsa

This recipe is one I found on a 4chan thread. I was browsing the /x/ board and one post said "This thread is boring, here's a Salsa Recipe". I appreciated that, so I decided to save it.

The name does not lie, this salsa is SPICY, and will leave your stomach hurting something fierce if you eat too much of it. Proceed with caution.

Ingredients

  • 4 roma tomatoes, cut in half
  • 1 white onion, cut in fourths
  • 1 whole garlic head, 1/3 of top cut off
  • 3 fresh jalapeno peppers, cut in half
  • 3 fresh serrano peppers, cut in half
  • 3 dried arbol peppers, whole
  • 1 bunch of cilantro, bottom half cut off
  • 1-2 limes
  • sea salt and olive oil, preheat oven to 450

Preparation Steps

  1. Place tomatoes, onions, garlic head, and all peppers onto an oven safe pan drizzle oil over everything and generously sprinkle salt over everything. place in oven for about 25 minutes
  2. Set garlic to side to cool. place everything into a blender. Use a fork to remove garlic cloves from skins. Add 1 shot of oil 1-2 pinches of salt, limes squeezed and the cilantro. Blend.
  3. 1 batch = 1 mason jar. discard after one week.
