Buffalo Sauce

This is literally just Buffalo Sauce like I don't know what else you could want me to say here. It tastes good.

Ingredients

  • 300g or 1 1/3c franks hot sauce (or brand of your choice)
  • 15g or 3-4 cloves minced garlic
  • 20g or 1Tbsp worcestershire
  • 15g or 1Tbsp white distilled vinegar
  • 150g or 10 1/2Tbsp unsalted butter, cubed
  • Very small pinch xanthan gum (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Add all ingredients except butter and xanthan to sauce pan over medium high heat.
  2. Whisk ingredients together and allow to cook for 4-5min until thickened slightly.
  3. Whisk in butter.
  4. Whisk in xanthan gum.
